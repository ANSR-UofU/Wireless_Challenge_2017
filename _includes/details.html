S.I.T.E.Packs will contain:

  • two Raspberry Pis,
  • two Sitara boards (Sitara is a custom RF transceiver that we are building based on TI CC1190 and TI CC1200, that operates in the 902-928 MHz ISM band and can communicate about 20-40 km),
  • extra Sitara boards (for volunteers/rescue personnel so that they can relay status/damage information back to the shelter quickly),
  • one or more usb battery packs which can be charged while discharging,
  • one or more solar panels that can charge the battery packs, and
  • corresponding cables and accessories.

With the exception of the Sitara boards, all the hardwares are readily available commercially.
